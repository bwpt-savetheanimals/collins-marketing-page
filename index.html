Why Help?

It's estimated that, because of human activities, current species extinction rates are around 1000 times greater than the background extinction rate (extinction rate that occurs without additional influence). Out of all species assessed, over 27,000 are at risk of extinction and should be under conservation.

Save The Animals aims to bridge the gap between concerned people such as yourselves and the animals in need.

How We Help

Save The Animals is a crowd-sourcing application for Animal Conservation. We have a two-pronged approach to this. First, we find conservation projects that are aligned to the user interests and concerns and connect them. Secondly, we approach the issue from the opposite side and allow conservation groups to broadcast their outreach to users and accept donations.
